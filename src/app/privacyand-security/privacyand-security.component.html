Privacy and Security Statement

Thank you for choosing to use our Angular application to store and manage artist information. We attach great importance to your privacy and security, therefore, we are committed to protecting the security of your personal information and data.

1. Data encryption and storage

For the artist information you input, we will strictly follow industry guidelines for encryption processing. In the process of data transmission and storage, we use advanced encryption technology to ensure that your information is maximally protected.

2. Principle of data minimization

We adhere to the principle of data minimization, only collecting necessary information related to artist information management, and minimizing storage volume as much as possible. Meanwhile, we will regularly review and remove unnecessary information to ensure that your data is not excessively retained.

3. Secure communication

The communication between this application and the server adopts HTTPS encryption protocol to ensure the security of data during transmission. Someone else has tampered with or intercepted your data.

4. Database integrity review

We regularly conduct database integrity reviews to check the integrity and security of the database. We will investigate potential security risks and promptly fix any issues found to ensure the security of your data.

5. Compliance with privacy laws and regulations

We strictly abide by relevant privacy laws and regulations, respect and protect your personal privacy rights.

6. Security vulnerability monitoring

We have adopted an advanced security vulnerability monitoring system. Once the system detects any abnormalities or potential risks, we will immediately take necessary measures to repair and prevent them.
